Join Europe Remotely Community

As remote developers, we face a lot of different types of issues daily. Finding clients, making sure that everything we do is compliant with the law, resolving difficult technical problems and trying to balance personal and professional lives are a daily thing.

Working from Europe introduces unique challenges, given that most of the countries don't recognize freelancers as a legal entity starting your remote career is often difficult. We have always relied on helping each other out to get our business running.

That's why we decided to join forces and start a slack community. Not only to support each other but also to make working remotely in Europe a better experience.
